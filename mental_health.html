How to Maintain Good Mental Health

Mental health is just as important as physical health. Here are some tips:

  • Practice Mindfulness: Engage in mindfulness or meditation to reduce stress.
  • Stay Connected: Maintain strong relationships with friends and family.
  • Get Enough Sleep: Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night.
  • Exercise Regularly: Physical activity can greatly benefit mental well-being.
  • Seek Help When Needed: Don’t hesitate to talk to a mental health professional if you’re struggling.
