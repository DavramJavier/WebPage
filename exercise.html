How to Make Your Body Healthier Through Exercise

Regular exercise is crucial for maintaining good health. Here are some tips to get started:

  • Set Realistic Goals: Start with achievable goals and gradually increase your intensity.
  • Mix It Up: Include a variety of activities like cardio, strength training, and flexibility exercises.
  • Stay Consistent: Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity each week.
  • Listen to Your Body: Pay attention to how you feel during and after exercise.
  • Enjoy the Process: Find activities that you enjoy to make exercise more enjoyable.
